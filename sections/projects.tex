  \item \projectSubheading
    {SNN Model of Hippocampal Reverse Replay}
    {MSc Dissertation}
    {March 2025 -- Sept. 2025}
  \itemizeBegin
    \item Reimplemented an existing rate-based robotic model of hippocampal reverse replay for reinforcement learning as a biologically plausible spiking neural network (SNN)
    \item Independently acquired working knowledge of neurophysiological principles including eligibility traces, intrinsic plasticity, synaptic dynamics, and short-term plasticity, all of which were entirely new at the outset of the project
    \item Completed within 6 months with no prior background in computational neuroscience, applying concepts from scratch to produce a functional biologically plausible implementation
  \itemizeEnd
